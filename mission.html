About Wicked Food Services

Our Story

Wicked Food Services was born out of a deep-seated passion for the art of cooking and the diversity of flavors that make every meal a unique experience. Our founder, Joe, hails from the breathtaking landscapes of Mongolia, a place where tradition and culture are as rich and diverse as the ingredients that grace our plates.

Our Mission

At Wicked Food Services, our mission is simple yet profound: to share the love and flavors of Mongolia with the world. We believe that everyone deserves a taste of the extraordinary, and we're committed to making that a reality for our customers. With every dish we create, we aim to transport your taste buds on an adventure, leaving you with an unforgettable culinary experience.

Our Values

  • Passion: We're driven by an insatiable passion for food, always seeking out the best and most flavorful ingredients to create extraordinary dishes.
  • Authenticity: We honor the authenticity of Mongolian cuisine, ensuring that each dish is prepared with traditional techniques and love.
  • Quality: Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We source the finest ingredients and craft each dish with care and precision.
  • Community: Food has a unique ability to bring people together, and we're dedicated to fostering a community of food enthusiasts who appreciate the richness of diverse flavors.

Our Offerings

Wicked Food Services is more than just a restaurant; we're an experience. Our menu showcases the exquisite tastes of Mongolia, with a modern twist. Whether you're craving hearty and savory dishes, or you have a taste for something sweet, we've got something wickedly delicious for everyone. Join us on this culinary adventure and explore the incredible world of Mongolian cuisine.
